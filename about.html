About me

My name is Isabel Maria Guner-Velasco and I am a professional musician turned software developer.

I have always been interested in using ICT resources when teaching music because the Internet and new technologies offer fantastic tools that I could use for the benefit and enjoyment of my students.

Being an end user, however, was not enough for me and I wanted to see how technology works behind the scenes. This curiosity combined with the circumstances of the pandemic contributed to my decision to switch careers and learn coding.

In the last two years I contributed to two comercial websites. currently enrolled in the Front-end development program at Noroff: School of technology and digital media.
